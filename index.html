MEET PRISCILA FERREIRA

As an authentic Brazilian dance instructor, performer, and choreographer based in Minnesota, Priscila is dedicated to preserving and celebrating the joy, complexity, and liberating expression of Samba.

Latin Dance MN serves as a vibrant cultural bridge, connecting dancers of all backgrounds, levels, and ages to the syncopated rhythms of Brazil. From fundamental footwork to spectacular carnival staging, Priscila provides supportive, high-energy coaching that empowers you to dance with freedom, confidence, and authentic style.
